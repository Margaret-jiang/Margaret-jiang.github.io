                                                          The results of career planning test


By taking the Myers-Briggs test, I learned that my personality can be described as "protagonist" and that my strengths are: Tolerant, Reliable, Charismatic, Altruistic, Natural, Leaders and weaknesses are: Overly Idealistic, Too Selfless, Too Sensitive, Fluctuating Self-Esteem, Struggle to Make Tough Decisions.

I have always been an extrovert and have often made decisive choices in a group. Being too idealistic is really my biggest weakness, and this is something I need to pay attention to in the future. Through the learning style test, I should focus more on visual memory in my future studies, and the career plan is to clarify my love for creativity.

Through these three tests, I think that when I work in a group, I should provide more creative ideas, to drive the whole group atmosphere through my own strength, and most importantly, to record more details to improve the quality of the whole group's work. And I should not ignore the feelings of other group members, and we should discuss more with each member of the group when making some decisions, to hear different voices.

When forming a team, I will do my best to bring out my own strengths, hoping that everyone has different personalities, everyone can have what they are good at and what they are not good at, we can help each other and provide different opinions to make the whole team more harmonious and complete.
